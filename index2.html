Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • 3/4 cup warm milk
  • 2 1/4 teaspons yeast
  • 1/4 cup granulatedsugar
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • 1/4 cup of butter
  • 3 cup bread flour

For the filling

  1. 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  2. 1 1/2 tablespons ground Cinnamon
  3. 1/4 cup butter

Instructions

  • Mix the milk with the yeast, sugar,eggs.
  • Melt the butter and add to the mixture.
  • add the flour and mix until combined into a dough.
  • Knead the dough for 10 minuites.
  • Transver the dough into bowl and cover with plastic wrap.Leave it some ware to rise for 2 hours.
  • After the dough has double in size , roll it out into a large rectanger.
